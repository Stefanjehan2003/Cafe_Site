Our Story

Harbor Street TheCafe celebrates 25 years this November 2023. Founded in 1998 by chef Liam O'Connor, TheCafe started as a small bistro in Silverport and quickly became a beloved spot for its unique blend of global flavors and local charm. Known for its rustic ambiance and rotating art exhibits, TheCafe has become a cultural hub where food and creativity meet. Over the years, TheCafe has grown into an iconic gathering place, bringing people together over good food and great conversations. As we celebrate this milestone, we invite you to be part of our journey—a place where every visit is a cherished memory.
